Tired of using guitar capo charts? This calculator replaces all of them.

Simply enter the root of the chord you want to play (for example, an A# minor chord would correspond to just "A sharp") in the Chord Shape field, select the Capo Fret, and click the "Find Chord Sound" button on the right side. Then try any other combination! Fill out the fields for the other two fields in order to find the missing piece!
